Pulao Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Vegitables
  • Garam Masala

Instructions

  1. Take all the ingrediants as per taste required.
  2. Add copped vegitables in the Cooker, Add salt and spice as per taste and fry it.
  3. Now add the rice and water and boild the rice. And your Swadist(Tasty) Pulao is ready.
